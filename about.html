About Our University

Excellence in Information and Communication Education since 2009

2009
Established

Leading the Digital Revolution in Zambia

Information and Communication University (ICU Zambia) is a private higher education institution located in Lusaka, Zambia.

Founded in 2009, ICU Zambia is officially recognized by the Ministry of Education of Zambia and the Higher Education Authority (HEA). We are dedicated to providing quality education in information and communication technologies, preparing students for the digital economy.
