About ICU Zambia

Excellence in Information and Communication Education

2009
Established

Leading the Digital Revolution in Zambia

Information and Communication University (ICU Zambia) is a private higher education institution located in Lusaka, Zambia [citation:1].

Founded in 2009, ICU Zambia is officially recognized by the Ministry of Education of Zambia and the Higher Education Authority (HEA) [citation:1]. We are dedicated to providing quality education in information and communication technologies, preparing students for the digital economy.
